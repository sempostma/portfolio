Problem & Approach

Patients use ChatGPT.
It sounds right. It isn’t.

“I asked ChatGPT — it’s fast, comfortable, no login needed.”

That’s the bar we must match. If our tool is harder to reach, patients go back to ChatGPT — where answers are confidently wrong.

User

Patient looking for information — in a format as fast and familiar as ChatGPT, but correct. Like thuisarts.nl, but for oncology. On the IKNL page.

●  Familiar & frictionless

No login. No navigation. A chatbot — because that’s where the problem lives and what patients already use.

●  Accurate — sources only

Direct links to trusted sources. No interpretation, no clinical decisions. If we’re not sure, we say so.

●  Quantitative

Digitized Lastmeter detects psychosocial need & creates population data for IKNL. Output exports as a GP-ready summary.

Impact

Three groups benefit.
One tool.

👤

Patient

Correct, cited answers — as fast as ChatGPT, no login. Distress screening exports as a GP-ready summary, so patients can fully describe their situation before a time-limited appointment.

The chat becomes a helper — not a replacement for their doctor.

👔

Clinician

Structured distress report arrives before the visit. Patients are better prepared. Clinical visit anxiety — rushing to describe everything in 10 minutes — is reduced.

📊

IKNL

Aggregated Lastmeter scores over time create a new population signal. If average distress rises year over year, IKNL sees it in the data before it appears in clinical records.
